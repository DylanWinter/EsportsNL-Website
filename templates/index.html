Frequently Asked Questions

What games are played?

So far, we have held events in Counter-Strike and Rocket League. However, we are open to all games.

Can I host a tournament?

Esports NL is designed as a 'central hub' for players to organize their own events. If you want to have a tournament for a game that isn't currently being played, reach out to us!

Are events in person or online?

Both! We have held both online tournaments and local LAN events.

Is there prize money?

Depends on the event. Most of our tournaments are for bragging rights only, but we have held tournaments with prize pools of up to $500 CAD.

Is it free to participate?

Again, this depends on the event. However, nearly all are completely free to enter.

I live outside NL. Can I still participate?

Yes! Our rule for online tournaments is that at least half of your roster must be NL-based. This means that for a team of 5, at least 3 players must be local.
